About April 2025

April is the fourth month of the year and is known for its pleasant spring weather. It is a time of renewal and growth.

Special Events in Nepal:

  • April 14: Nepali New Year (Bikram Sambat 2082)
  • April 22: Earth Day
  • April 25: Anniversary of the 2015 Nepal Earthquake
